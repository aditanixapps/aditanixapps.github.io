Privacy Policy — Ezkeep

Effective date: 17 July 2026  •  App: Ezkeep — Stock & Billing (com.aditanix.ezkeep)
Developer: Aditanix Technologies  •  Contact: support@aditanix.com

The short version

Ezkeep is a 100% offline business tool. Your products, customers, suppliers, sales, purchases, payments and quotations are stored only on your device. We run no servers, require no account, and never see, collect, upload or sell your business data. The only third-party service in the app is Google AdMob, which shows advertisements.

1. Data you create in the app

All records you enter (inventory, parties, transactions, business profile, logo, settings) are saved in a local database on your phone. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to share it — for example by sending an invoice PDF on WhatsApp, exporting a CSV, or saving a backup file to your own Google Drive or email. Files you share or back up are governed by the service you send them to.

2. Permissions we request and why

  • Camera — used only while you scan a product barcode. No photos or video are captured, stored, or transmitted.
  • Notifications — used for optional local reminders (overdue customer payments and a weekly backup nudge). These are generated on your device; you can disable them in the app's Settings or system settings.
  • Internet — used solely to load advertisements and the ad-consent form. Your business data is never transmitted.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Ezkeep is free and supported by ads served by Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, Google may collect device information such as the advertising ID, IP address, coarse device data, and ad-interaction events. Where required (e.g. EEA/UK), a consent form is shown and you can change your choice anytime in Settings → Privacy Options inside the app. Learn more: How Google uses ad data · Google Privacy Policy. You can also reset or delete your advertising ID in your device's Google settings.

4. What we do NOT do

  • No account creation, no login, no phone-number collection by us.
  • No upload of your business records to any server.
  • No sale or sharing of your data with data brokers.
  • No access to your contacts, SMS, location, or files beyond what you explicitly pick (e.g. choosing a logo image or a backup file to restore).

5. Data retention & deletion

Your data stays on your device for as long as you keep the app. Deleting records in-app removes them from the local database. Uninstalling Ezkeep permanently deletes the app's local database from your device. Backup files you exported remain wherever you saved them and are yours to delete.

6. Children

Ezkeep is a business utility intended for adults and is not directed at children under 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your region).

7. Security

Data is stored in your device's private app storage, protected by Android's application sandbox. Protect your backup files as you would any business document.

8. Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.

9. Contact

Questions or requests: support@aditanix.com
