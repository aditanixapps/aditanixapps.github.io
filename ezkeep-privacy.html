Privacy Policy

Ezkeep — Easy Inventory Tracker

Effective date: 15-07-2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Ezkeep mobile application ("the App"), published by Aditanix Technologies ("we", "us", "our"), handles information when you use the App. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Ezkeep is an offline-first application. All business data you enter in the App is stored only in a local database on your own device and is never transmitted to us or to any server we operate. This includes:

  • Products, categories, stock quantities, and prices
  • Sales, purchases, and payment records
  • Customer and supplier names, phone numbers, and addresses that you choose to enter

We have no access to this data. It never leaves your device unless you yourself export and share it (see Section 5). If you uninstall the App, this data is deleted along with it.

2. Advertising and Automatically Collected Data

The App displays advertisements served by Google AdMob, an advertising service provided by Google LLC. To serve and measure ads, Google AdMob may automatically collect certain information from your device, including:

  • Device advertising identifier (Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address and coarse (approximate) location derived from it
  • Device information such as model, operating system version, and language
  • Ad interaction data such as impressions and clicks, and diagnostic data

This collection is performed by Google under its own privacy policy, available at https://policies.google.com/privacy. Information about how Google uses data from apps that use its services is available at https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites. You can reset or delete your Android Advertising ID at any time in your device settings (Settings > Google > Ads).

We do not collect, sell, or share your personal information ourselves. We do not operate any servers that receive data from the App.

3. Consent for Personalized Ads (EEA, UK, and US States)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, the App will show you a consent form on first launch, powered by Google's User Messaging Platform, before any advertising data is processed. You may consent to personalized ads, choose non-personalized ads, or manage individual purposes. You can change your choice at any time using the "Privacy Options" button in the App's Settings screen.

If you are located in a US state with applicable privacy laws, a corresponding privacy message may be shown allowing you to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information for advertising.

4. Camera Permission

The App requests the CAMERA permission solely to scan product barcodes so you can add and find products quickly. Camera frames are processed on your device in real time using Google ML Kit and are not stored, recorded, or transmitted anywhere. You can deny or revoke this permission at any time; the App remains fully usable without it, except for the barcode scanning feature.

5. Data Export and Sharing

The App lets you export your inventory data to a CSV file. This export is created on your device and shared only through apps you explicitly choose (such as your email, cloud storage, or messaging apps). Once shared, the handling of that file is governed by the privacy policy of the app or service you shared it with.

6. Children

The App is a business tool intended for adults and is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Advertising in the App is not configured for child-directed treatment.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Because your business data exists only on your device, you are in full control of it. You can delete individual records inside the App, clear the App's data from your device settings, or uninstall the App to remove all locally stored data. Data collected by Google AdMob is retained according to Google's retention policies; see Google's privacy policy for details.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights such as access, correction, deletion, and objection regarding personal data. For data held on your device, you can exercise these directly in the App. For data processed by Google for advertising, please refer to Google's privacy controls at https://myadcenter.google.com and https://policies.google.com/privacy.

9. Security

Your business data is stored in the App's private storage area, which is protected by the Android operating system and not accessible to other apps on standard devices. We recommend protecting your device with a screen lock.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
