Privacy Policy — EzSplit

Effective date: 24 July 2026  •  App: EzSplit: Trip & Group Splitter (com.aditanix.ezsplit)
Developer: Aditanix Technologies  •  Contact: support@aditanix.com

The short version

EzSplit is a 100% offline expense-splitting tool. Your groups, members, expenses, splits, settlements and budgets are stored only on your device. We run no servers, require no account, and never see, collect, upload or sell your data. The only third-party service in the app is Google AdMob, which shows advertisements.

1. Data you create in the app

All records you enter (groups, members, expenses, splits, settlements, budgets, settings) are saved in a local database on your phone. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to share it — for example by sending a PDF statement or WhatsApp summary, exporting a CSV, or saving a backup file to your own Google Drive or email. Files you share or back up are governed by the service you send them to. Foreign-currency amounts and exchange rates you enter for offline multi-currency expenses are calculated on your device and never transmitted anywhere.

2. Permissions we request and why

  • Notifications — used for an optional weekly local reminder to back up your data. This is generated on your device; you can disable it in the app's Settings.
  • Internet — used solely to load advertisements and the ad-consent form. Your group and expense data is never transmitted.

EzSplit does not request Camera, Contacts, SMS, or Location access. The one-tap payment reminder feature opens your device's normal share sheet with a pre-filled message — you choose who to send it to (e.g. via WhatsApp); EzSplit never reads your contacts.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

EzSplit is free and supported by ads served by Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, Google may collect device information such as the advertising ID, IP address, coarse device data, and ad-interaction events. Where required (e.g. EEA/UK), a consent form is shown and you can change your choice anytime in Settings → Ad privacy options inside the app. Learn more: How Google uses ad data · Google Privacy Policy. You can also reset or delete your advertising ID in your device's Google settings.

4. What we do NOT do

  • No account creation, no login, no phone-number collection by us.
  • No upload of your groups, expenses, or balances to any server.
  • No sale or sharing of your data with data brokers.
  • No access to your contacts, SMS, location, or files beyond what you explicitly pick (e.g. choosing a backup file to restore).

5. Data retention & deletion

Your data stays on your device for as long as you keep the app. Deleting a group or expense in-app removes it from the local database. Uninstalling EzSplit permanently deletes the app's local database from your device. Backup files you exported remain wherever you saved them and are yours to delete.

6. Children

EzSplit is a general-audience utility intended for adults and is not directed at children under 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your region).

7. Security

Data is stored in your device's private app storage, protected by Android's application sandbox. Protect your backup files as you would any personal financial document.

8. Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.

9. Contact

Questions or requests: support@aditanix.com
